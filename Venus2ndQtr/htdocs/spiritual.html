Mental Health: Why is it important to tackle?

Spiritual life is an aspect of your life that can let us talk to God. Also, it gives us the oppurtunity to get more closer to God. Spiritual life is the most important aspect in our life. Healthy spirituality gives a sense of peace, wholeness and balance among the physical, emotional, social and spiritual aspects of our lives. However, for most people the path to such spirituality passes through struggles and suffering, and often includes experiences that are frightening and painful.

The difference between the Physical and Spiritual Life:

The spiritual world is the eternal, infinite plane of bliss and knowledge. The material world is the plane of matter, the plane of birth, disease, old age, and death. The spiritual world is home to the soul. The material plane is home to the body and mind.
